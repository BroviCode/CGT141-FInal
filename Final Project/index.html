Welcome to HCA


HCA (Human Created Art) is a website designed to help people get AI free art for whatever they need! Instead of generating art through AI, you can easily commision artists for free to create any design or image.


Features

Submit requests: Fill out what you desire and your requirements

Track Status: Monitor the status of your submissions in real-time on the Status page.

Library: Access other peoples submissions to use for free.


Getting Started

To get started, navigate to the Submit page to upload your ideas. After submission, you can check its status on the Status page and view all submissions in the Library.
